-BORACAY-

Boracay is a tropical island of The Philippines. It is belong to Malay Community in the Province of Aklan. It is also devided in three different districts, like Barangay Balabag, Mano-Manoc and Yapak. Tourists from all over the World are attracts to Boracay's white sandy, beaches and crystal clear water in lovely turquoise color and in addition to that, there are so many activities you can do. Like diving, snorkeling and many many more.
